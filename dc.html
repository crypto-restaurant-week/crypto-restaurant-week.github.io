About

  • Who We Are

    ACTx is a brand strategy, global marketing and product onboarding company. We help startups and established companies achieve their business goals. We're passionate about technology and its potential to solve problems, improve lives, and change the world.

  • What We Do

    ACTx designs and executes launch strategies for decentralized, Web3 technologies. We combine design thinking, experiential marketing and partnership strategies to execute growth campaigns that are highly localized and user-centric. Our blockchain tech focused team was formed in 2018, and has been working with protocols, builders, and creatives ever since.

  • What is an ACTivation?

    ACTivations are onboarding campaigns models. Combining end-user testing and localization strategies, these models are built to be replicated, generating real-time insights for product builders.
